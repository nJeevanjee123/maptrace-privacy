How do I trace a path on the map?

Simply drag your finger along the desired route on the map. The app will automatically create a traced path following your finger movement.

How do I view elevation profiles?

After tracing a path, tap the "Elevation Profile" button to see a detailed chart showing elevation changes along your route.

How do I switch between metric and imperial units?

Go to Settings and toggle between Metric (km/m) and Imperial (mi/ft) units. The change applies immediately to all measurements.

Why does the app request location permission?

Location permission is only used to center the map on your current position when you tap the location button. No location data is stored or transmitted.

Does the app work offline?

You can trace paths offline, but internet connection is required to load map tiles and elevation data from Google Maps.

How do I clear traced paths?

Use the "Clear All" button in the app to remove all traced paths and start fresh.
