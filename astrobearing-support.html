‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate bearings?

Tap the compass button to place a reference peg, then move the map to see live bearing and distance calculations between the peg and pen.

How do I track sun and moon positions?

Tap the sun button to cycle through modes: Sun mode shows sunrise/sunset positions, Moon mode shows moonrise/moonset. Use the time wheel to explore positions at different times.

How do I switch between True and Magnetic North?

Go to Settings (gear icon) to toggle between True North and Magnetic North modes.

How do I change units?

Go to Settings and toggle between Metric (km/m) and Imperial (mi/ft) units.

Why does the app need location permission?

Location is only used to center the map on your current position and calculate accurate sun/moon positions. No location data is stored or transmitted.

How do I view elevation profiles?

Draw a path with the pen tool, then tap the mountain (elevation) button to see the elevation profile along your path.
