🏔️ MapTrace+ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

📱 About MapTrace+

MapTrace+ is a professional mapping and elevation analysis tool for iOS. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

📍 Location Data Collection

MapTrace+ accesses your device location only to:

  • Center the map on your current position when you tap the location button
  • Display your current location on the map for navigation reference
  • Provide location-based mapping and navigation features

🔒 Data Storage and Privacy

We prioritize your privacy:

  • No location data is stored on our servers or transmitted to us
  • No personal information is collected or shared with third parties
  • All traced paths and analysis remain on your device only
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No tracking or analytics of your usage patterns

🗺️ Third-Party Services

MapTrace+ uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps: For map display and elevation data
  • Google's data handling is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Elevation queries are sent to Google's servers but contain no personal information

🛡️ Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Location permissions can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings
  • All app data is deleted when you delete the app
  • No account deletion needed since no accounts are created

👶 Children's Privacy

MapTrace+ does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children specifically.

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
